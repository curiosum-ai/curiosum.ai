The AI Age? No — the Age of Curiosity.

Tools always get misnamed as ages. Steam was called the Machine Age — it was the age of humans coordinating production. The internet was called the Information Age — it was the age of humans trading in knowledge. AI is being called the AI Age. It is the Age of Curiosity.

The AI Age is not an age. It is a toolset. Naming it an age is the mistake of the people selling the tools.
